Behind the scenes

Polaris has always been a symbol of hope—a pillar of direction in an unfamiliar world—and the United States has lost its way. Current US politics are on cyclical descent. Party Polaris will break the cycle— prioritizing people over politics. We will facilitate a shift to sustainable energy sources, revamp aging infrastructure, and provide comprehensive support systems for Americans who have been long undervalued by biased institutions. Abroad, we will promote stability through peaceful resolutions to decade-old conflicts while maintaining relations with our strategic allies. The journey towards progress is long and dark—but Polaris will light the way forward.
